Provision Organization Controller

This will issue a RADIUS secret and EAP-TLS certificate for the CSH WiFi controller. The credentials will be displayed once — save them immediately.

The client will be stored under the reserved username root and is not linked to any member account.

Regenerate — Organization Controller

This will revoke the current certificate and issue a new RADIUS secret and cert for the CSH WiFi controller. The new credentials will be displayed once.

The controller will lose WiFi access until it is reconfigured with the new credentials.

Deprovision — Organization Controller

This will revoke the certificate and remove all RADIUS credentials for the CSH WiFi controller.

The controller will immediately stop accepting CSH WiFi logins.
